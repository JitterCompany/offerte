# Jitter B.V. - Company Profile

_The technical partner for companies developing smart hardware_

## Overview

Jitter is an engineering company with deep expertise in electronics, firmware, and software. We partner with innovative companies as their extended technical team, helping them build and evolve complex hardware products. Founded in 2015 and based in Delft, we bring over 10 years of hands-on product development experience.

**Our Value:** Unlike typical contractors, we become part of your team. Our greatest value emerges in long-term partnerships where we provide technical continuity as your product evolves and your team changes. We often end up knowing more about a client's product than they do, because we remain constant while their teams grow and shift.

---

## What We Do

### Embedded Systems
Hardware and firmware designed as one integrated whole. We co-design electronics and firmware from specification through production transfer, eliminating the integration problems that arise when hardware and software are developed separately.

### PCB Design
Custom electronics design from concept through production-ready files. Sensor integration, mixed-signal designs, low-power battery systems, and industrial-grade applications. We use KiCad for all PCB work.

### Embedded Firmware
Reliable, maintainable firmware in Rust (preferred for new projects) and C. From bare-metal to async architectures, including motor control, sensor processing, communication protocols, and embedded GUIs.

### Modernizing Legacy Systems
Step-by-step upgrades for systems where components are no longer available, documentation has been lost, or original engineers have left. We deliver drop-in replacements that maintain backward compatibility while enabling new features — no big-bang rewrites, minimal downtime.

### Embedded GUI & Touchscreens
Industrial interfaces, medical displays, and sensor dashboards. We build on Raspberry Pi/Linux for complex interfaces or bare-metal firmware for simpler displays, with full hardware integration included.

### PCB Design Review
Independent review of existing designs covering schematic choices, layout, power integrity, signal integrity, and manufacturability. Concrete feedback before you commit to fabrication.

### Custom Projects
Multi-disciplinary projects combining hardware, firmware, software, and mechanics. We act as a seamless extension of your team with a single point of contact across all disciplines.

## Selected Projects

**Frogwatch** — Structural health monitoring platform built from the ground up: custom sensor hardware, embedded firmware, cloud backend, and analytics dashboard. Now spun off as an independent company ([frog.watch](https://www.frog.watch)). Demonstrates our capability to build complete end-to-end products.

**DoseRx1** — GMP-compliant 3D printer for personalized medication, developed over 2.5 years as an integrated part of the Doser team. Distributed CAN bus architecture, embedded Rust firmware, and Angular-based touchscreen interface.

**Operation Air** — Emergency ventilator developed in 3 weeks during COVID-19 in collaboration with TU Delft. Complete control electronics, two PCB revisions, and real-time embedded GUI. 80 units commissioned by the Dutch Ministry of Health.

## Why Partner with Jitter
